# KalyTrex

**The Human Harmony** — Secure. Intelligent. Human-Centric.  
We build ethical, non-addictive digital platforms that people can trust.

KalyTrex is a product studio focused on designing and engineering privacy-preserving, security-led, and UX-first applications. Our goal is to raise the standard for trust, safety, and mindful technology use—without compromising user ownership rights.

## What we’re building

### ✅ SafePlay (In Progress)
A child safety platform for digital gaming:
- Age policy & guardian controls
- Behavior monitoring and safety signals
- Real-time moderation support (chat/voice/interaction)
- Parent/guardian visibility and reporting

### 🔒 Private AI Assistant (Planned)
A privacy-preserving AI assistant concept (name currently in progress), designed for:
- Trust-first personalization
- Secure deployment options

### 🧠 WristIt (SafeBand) (Discovery)
A wearable safety concept focused on discreet alerting and safety intelligence.
